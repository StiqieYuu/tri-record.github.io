Founding & Objective

ABOUT US

Our vinyl record company was founded in 2019 with the goal of providing music fans with the highest quality vinyl records and a wide selection of genres to choose from. We believe that the vinyl format provides a superior listening experience and want to help keep this format alive and thriving. In addition to offering a wide range of records, we also strive to create a welcoming and inclusive community for music lovers of all ages. Whether you're a seasoned collector or new to the world of vinyl, we hope to provide you with a enjoyable shopping experience and help you discover new and exciting music. Our ultimate objective is to become the go-to destination for vinyl enthusiasts everywhere and help keep the love of vinyl alive for generations to come.

Partner With Us

ABOUT US

Are you a record label, artist, or other industry professional looking to partner with our vinyl record company? We're always on the lookout for new and exciting opportunities to collaborate and help bring great music to our customers. Here are just a few ways we can work together: Record releases: If you have an upcoming album or reissue on vinyl, we'd love to hear from you. We offer a range of distribution and marketing services to help get your music into the hands of vinyl collectors. Artist merchandise: Do you have a line of merchandise that you'd like to sell through our online store? We can help you reach a wider audience and take care of fulfillment and customer service. In-store events: Are you touring and looking for a place to hold an in-store performance or meet-and-greet? Our store is the perfect spot for intimate, exclusive events. Promotions: Do you have a new single, video, or tour coming up that you'd like to promote? We have a range of marketing channels that we can use to help get the word out. If you're interested in partnering with us, please don't hesitate to get in touch. We'd love to hear from you and explore ways we can work together.
