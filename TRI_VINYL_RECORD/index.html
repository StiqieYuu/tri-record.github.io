Vinyl Record Company

TRI Record Company is a business that specializes in selling a wide variety of vinyl records. From classic rock and pop albums to jazz and blues recordings, TRI has something for every music enthusiast. Our extensive collection includes rare and hard-to-find releases, as well as new pressings of popular albums. In addition to offering a diverse selection of records, TRI Record Company is also dedicated to providing top-quality customer service. Our knowledgeable staff is always ready to help customers find the perfect record or answer any questions they may have. We also offer a hassle-free return policy, so customers can shop with confidence. Whether you're a seasoned vinyl collector or new to the world of analog audio, TRI Record Company is the perfect place to find the perfect record. So come on down and browse our selection today!
