Trade In

SERVICES

Our vinyl record company is proud to offer a trade-in program for our customers. If you have any vinyl records that you no longer want or need, you can trade them in for store credit. All you need to do is fill out a simple form on our website. Simply provide us with some basic information about the records you want to trade in, and we'll give you an estimate of the store credit you'll receive. Our trade-in program is a convenient way for you to declutter your collection and get some new records in the process. Plus, you'll be supporting a small business and helping to keep the vinyl industry alive and well.

Pre-Release Sample

SERVICES

Are you a vinyl collector who loves getting your hands on the latest releases before anyone else? If so, our pre-release sample program might be just what you're looking for. We offer a select group of customers the opportunity to receive pre-release samples of upcoming vinyl releases. All you need to do is fill out a simple form on our website expressing your interest in participating in the program. If you're accepted, you'll receive exclusive access to pre-release samples, giving you the chance to preview and purchase records before they're available to the general public. Don't miss out on this exciting opportunity to be one of the first to own the newest releases from your favorite artists.

Priority Gig Ticket

SERVICES

Are you a music lover who always wants to be first in line for the best gig tickets? If so, our priority gig tickets program is for you. As a member of this exclusive program, you'll receive advance notice of upcoming concerts and have the opportunity to purchase tickets before they're available to the general public. All you need to do is fill out a simple form on our website expressing your interest in participating. We'll then add you to our list of priority ticket holders and notify you when tickets for the shows you're interested in become available. Don't miss out on this chance to get the best seats in the house for all your favorite bands and artists.

Artist Meet and Greet

SERVICES

Are you a fan of a particular artist and dream of meeting them in person? If so, our artist meet & greet program might be just what you're looking for. We offer a select group of customers the opportunity to meet and greet their favorite artists. All you need to do is fill out a simple form on our website expressing your interest in participating in the program. If you're accepted, you'll have the chance to meet and chat with the artist, get a photo with them, and maybe even get an autograph. It's a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity for any true fan. Don't miss out on this chance to meet your idols and make unforgettable memories.
